Preparation

Elevate electrical appliances and furniture, create an emergency kit with essentials like food, water, and first aid, and stay updated on weather alerts through official sources.

Response

Move to higher ground immediately if flooding occurs, avoid walking or driving through floodwaters to prevent accidents, and follow evacuation orders from authorities.

Recovery

Assess damage safely once waters recede, clean and disinfect affected areas to prevent mold and disease, and seek disaster assistance if needed.
