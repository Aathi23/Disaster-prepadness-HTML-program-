Frequently Affected Areas: Bay of Bengal, Caribbean, Gulf of Mexico, Southeast Asia, Indian Ocean, Australia, Philippines, Japan, and the Eastern United States.

Preparation

Secure heavy furniture, fix loose objects, and create an emergency kit with food, water, and first aid. Identify safe spots like under sturdy tables.

Response

Drop to the ground, take cover under sturdy furniture, and hold on until shaking stops. Stay indoors and away from windows.

Recovery

Check for injuries, be prepared for aftershocks, and evacuate if buildings appear unsafe. Avoid power lines and check for structural damages.
